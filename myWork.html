Puppy Love

I worked on a team to build a mobile app for dog owners to find compatible dog playmates for their pup. My team and I worked as UX researchers and UI designers to come up with an app that allows users to swip yes or no on dogs they can match with. We conducted user interviews to seek understanding with our users, refined specific key features to focus on, and developed a high-fidelity mobile prototype.
